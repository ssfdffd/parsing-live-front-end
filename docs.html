Embedding Parsing™ Live on your website

You can easily embed our secure video rooms directly into your own website, intranet, or client portal using a simple HTML iframe.

Step 1: Choose your room

Decide on a unique room name for your integration (e.g., client-consultation).

Step 2: Copy the Embed Code

Use the following code snippet. Replace YOUR_ROOM_NAME with your chosen room name.

<iframe
  src="https://live.parsing.co.za/live.html?room=YOUR_ROOM_NAME"
  width="100%"
  height="600px"
  style="border: 2px solid #F07080; border-radius: 12px;"
  allow="camera; microphone; display-capture"
  title="Parsing Live WebRTC">
</iframe>

Important Notes

  • The allow="camera; microphone" attribute is strictly required for the browser to grant access.
  • Free embedded rooms are subject to the 1-hour time limit.
  • For white-label solutions without the Parsing branding, please contact our sales team for Premium access.
